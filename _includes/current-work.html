mmWave FMCW Radar Human Sensing

Building a human sensing system on a hardware-constrained single-chip mmWave FMCW radar. Raw ADC samples are collected directly from the chip, then processed to filter clutter and isolate the faint signals reflected off the human body. A machine learning model learns the mapping between these sparse radar observations and a high-resolution supervision sensor — enabling the low-cost radar to infer rich spatial information it could not produce alone. Conceptually similar to SitPose.
